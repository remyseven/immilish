💡 The short version: Immilish does not sell your data. We do not run ads. We do not require an account. Your learning is your own. The only data that leaves your device is the language questions you answer — sent to our AI to generate coaching feedback.

1

Who We Are

Immilish is an AI-powered language learning app created and operated by TYLM LLC. Immilish was designed and built by Tyler, a young entrepreneur with a passion for helping people communicate across languages and cultures.

When we say "Immilish," "we," "us," or "our" in this Privacy Policy, we mean TYLM LLC and the Immilish app.

📍 TYLM LLC is based in the United States. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Immilish app worldwide.

2

Information We Collect

Immilish is designed to collect as little information as possible. Here is exactly what is and is not collected:

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • An account login or password of any kind
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Payment information (the app is free)
  • Any personally identifiable information

What is sent to our AI service when you use the app:

  • The language you selected (e.g., "Spanish")
  • The level you are on (e.g., "Level 3 — Conversations")
  • The question prompt and your typed or selected answer

This information is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate a coaching response. It is not linked to your identity in any way.

⚠️ Important: Please do not type personal information (like your real name, address, or phone number) into any answer field in Immilish. Answer fields are for language learning responses only.

3

How We Use Information

The only information transmitted from the Immilish app is used solely to:

  • Generate AI-powered coaching feedback after each answer
  • Generate language learning questions for languages without a built-in question bank
  • Provide word definitions in the Tap-to-Define paragraph reader (Filipino Level 5)

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, analytics tracking, or selling to third parties. Ever.

✅ Your progress through levels (which levels you have completed) is stored only on your device using local memory. It never leaves your phone.

4

AI & Third-Party Services

Immilish uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power its coaching, question generation, and word definition features. When you interact with these features, your language learning inputs are sent to Anthropic's API over a secure HTTPS connection.

Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy. We encourage you to review it.

Immilish also loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is a standard web font service and does not involve any personal data from users.

We do not use:

  • Facebook SDK or any Meta services
  • Google Analytics or any tracking analytics
  • Advertising networks of any kind
  • Crash reporting services that collect device data
5

Data Storage & Security

Immilish does not operate servers or databases that store user data. All of your learning progress is stored locally on your device and is cleared when you uninstall the app.

All communication between the Immilish app and Anthropic's API is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS encryption.

We take the security of your data seriously and follow best practices to ensure that the minimal data we do transmit is protected in transit.

🔒 Because Immilish stores no personal data on our servers, there is nothing to breach. Your privacy is protected by design.

6

Children's Privacy

Immilish is rated Age 4+ and is designed to be safe and appropriate for users of all ages, including young children.

We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws worldwide. Specifically:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require any account creation or login
  • There are no social features, chat functions, or user-generated content
  • There are no in-app purchases or links to external websites
  • There is no advertising of any kind

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information through the app, please contact us immediately at the email address below and we will take prompt action.

🧒 Immilish was created by a young person, for people of all ages. We take children's safety and privacy more seriously than anything else.

7

Your Rights & Choices

Because Immilish does not collect or store personal data, most traditional data rights (such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data) are automatically satisfied — there is simply no personal data about you on our systems.

However, you have the following options:

  • Reset your progress: Uninstall and reinstall the app to clear all locally stored progress
  • Opt out of AI features: Simply do not use the app — all AI features require an internet connection and can be avoided by not using those features
  • GDPR rights (EU users): If you believe we hold any data about you and wish to exercise your rights under GDPR, contact us and we will respond within 30 days
  • CCPA rights (California users): We do not sell personal information. California users have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information — none of which applies since we don't collect or sell it
8

No Ads. Ever.

Immilish will never show advertisements. We will never sell your data to advertisers. We will never use your behavior to build advertising profiles.

Immilish is free to download because we believe language learning should be accessible to everyone — not because we are monetizing your attention.

🌟 No ads. No tracking. No selling your data. Just learning. That is the Immilish promise.

We will never make changes that reduce your privacy protections without providing clear notice.
